Why it works

Google is built for this. Most clinics just don't use it.

◈

Google prioritises real practitioners

Individual clinician profiles carry strong authority signals. A named consultant with a verified profile outranks a generic clinic listing more often than not.

⊞

More listings = more map coverage

Each profile is eligible to appear in local map packs independently. 10 clinicians means 10 chances to show up for "psychiatrist near me" or "private gynaecologist London".

★

Reviews become distributed trust signals

Instead of all reviews sitting on one profile, each clinician builds their own. That's stronger credibility — and more resilience if one clinician leaves.

The business case

When a clinician leaves, you're not left with nothing.

Most clinics don't think about what happens to the digital asset when a consultant moves on. With properly structured GBP profiles, you hold the value — not them.

Transfer the profile

When a clinician leaves, you can transfer their Google profile to their replacement — retaining the reviews, the ranking history, and the visibility you've built.

Or charge a handover fee

The profile has real commercial value. Some clinics charge departing clinicians a fee to take their profile with them. That's a legitimate income stream from SEO work you've already paid for.

You own the asset either way

We set up profiles under your clinic's ownership. The reviews, rankings, and data stay with your practice — not with the individual clinician or the agency.
